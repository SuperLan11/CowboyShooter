Sub-Room 1:
	Teaches movement, mainly having an open space to run around in, and a gap you have to wall jump across.

Sub-Room 2:
	Teaches the gun. After the player picks up the gun from a pedestal, there will be targets that the player has to shoot to unlock the ending door.

Sub-Room 3:
	Teaches the lasso. The player will get the lasso and have a gap they need to cross, that they cannot wall-jump on. There will be a hook on the ceiling that the player can use the lasso on.
